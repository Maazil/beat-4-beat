How a round works

Four beats to every game night

STEP 1

Build your board

Pick your categories and pull songs straight from your Spotify playlists. Set a point level on every track.

STEP 2

Pick a tile, hit play

Teams take turns choosing a category and a stake. The host controls the snippet — nobody touches the aux.

STEP 3

Shout it out

Title and artist score separately. Miss the artist? Another team can steal that point right off your round.

STEP 4

Reveal the standings

Totals stay hidden while you play. One reveal at the end, maximum drama, one winner.
